We’re here to improve patient care and health outcomes

NHSX is a new joint unit that will drive digital transformation in health and social care.

Background

The digital transformation of the NHS is the most important change we will make to healthcare for the next decade. But the responsibility for it is currently split between numerous organisations and teams.

We need a transformation in the way that technology is led in the NHS. NHSX brings together a new joint unit in the Department of Health and Social Care and NHS England to drive digital transformation and lead policy, implementation and change.

Find out about our background

“NHSX will combine some of the best minds from among the NHS, leading innovators, and government into one unit to set national policy, remove red tape and create a culture of innovation to allow the best innovations to flourish.”

Matt Hancock, Health Secretary

What we’re here to do

We’re here to improve patient care and outcomes by:

  • simplifying the organisational complexity
  • setting national policy and standards
  • working with and empowering the best digital minds across the NHS
  • creating an ecosystem where the latest digital innovations are made widely available

How we work

We are:

  • user centred and patient focused
  • open, transparent and want to engage
  • collaborative
  • supporting a culture of innovation
