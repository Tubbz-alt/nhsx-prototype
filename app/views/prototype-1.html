Mission and purpose

NHSX’s mission is to give patients and staff the technology they need, by:

  • Simplifying the organisational complexity
  • Setting national policy for NHS technology, digital and data
  • Developing guidance and mandating technical standards
  • Working with and empowering the best digital minds across the NHS
  • Creating an ecosystem where healthtech entrepreneurs make the latest digital innovations widely available

Ethos and principles

NHSX is user centred, patient focused and driven by delivering better care and outcomes for patients.

We believe:

  • Credible, authoritative voice on digital transformation in health and care
  • Open, transparent and engaged with the healthtech community
  • Change the discourse from ‘the NHS can’t do tech’ to ‘NHS tech is getting better’
  • Technology is worth the investment and we are serious about change: digital technology (digital first care) is key to a sustainable future NHS
